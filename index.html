HPV Simulator 2.0 has just been released, with lots of new features and bug fixes.

Welcome to the HPV Simulator

You have just found the website for the HPV Simulator - the best game you will ever find.
Here you can pointlessly drive a virtual Human Powered Vehicle around for as long as you like.

Human Powered Vehicles are recumbant tricycles equiped with an aerodynamic shell. They are much faster and more energy efficient than normal two wheeled bicycles.

The HPV Simulator is a small game made with the Unity Game Engine. Feel free to download the sourcecode and modify it to suit your needs and platforms. The game was put together in Unity 2017.1.1f1.
